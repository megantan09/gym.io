BODYWEIGHT EXERCISES

Best ways to target the muscles in your body without having to hit the gym!


PULL UPS

ACCESSIBILITY: Find pull up bars in your neighbourhood or obtain a portable pull up bar from your nearest sports store

Purchase a pull-up bar here.

Instructions:

3 sets of 5, rest between sets: 60s

1. Grab the bar with your palms facing away from you and your arms full extended

2. Hands should be shoulder width apart

3. Squeeze shoulder blades together, exhale and drive your elbows towards your hips to bring your chin above the bar

4. Lower under control back to the start position and repeat

Targets: Back (Latissimus Dorsi), Trapezius and Thoracic Erector Spinae

DIPS

ACCESSIBILITY: Use a chair at home or go to the nearest dip bar in neighbourhood

Instructions:

3 sets of 12 , rest between sets 60s

1. Grab dip bars with palms facing inward and your arms straight

2. Slowly lower until elbows are at right angles, ensuring they stay tucked against your body and donâ€™t flare out

3. Drive yourself back up to the top and repeat

Targets: Back (Pecs, Anterior Deltoids), Triceps and Biceps

PUSH UPS

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 10, rest between sets: 60s

1. Set up weight supported on your toes and hands beneath your shoulders, keeping body straight

2. Ensure your core is flexed so that your body remains straight throughout the exercise

3. Lower body until chest is an inch from the ground then push up, fully extending arms for the entire range of motion

Targets: Back (Deltoids), Chest (Pectorals), Shoulders and Triceps

PISTOL SQUAT

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 10, rest between sets: 30s

1. Stand with your arms straight out in front of your body at shoulder level, parallel to floor. Raise your right leg off the ground and hold it in front of you

2. Push hips back and lower your body as far as you can

3. Pause before pushing your body back to the starting position

Targets: Glutes, Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Hip Adductors, Calves and Core Muscles

STEP UP WITH KNEE RAISES

ACCESSIBILITY: Use staircases around you or a stool at home

Instructions:

3 sets of 10 on each leg, rest between sets: 60s

1. Place equipment in front of you and step onto it with 1 foot

2. As you plant your foot into the equipment, drive your other foot bringing your knee as high as you can

3. Lower back down and step back onto the floor

4. Repeat with other leg

Targets: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes, Hip Flexors and Rectus Abdominis

GROINERS

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 10, rest between sets: 30s

1. Start the exercise in a push up position

2. Jump forward so both legs land next to your hands

3. Return to starting position

Targets: Glutes, Hip Flexors and Hamstrings

SPIDER CRAWL

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 10, 5 on each side, rest between sets: 60s

1. From a push up position, raise one foot off the floor and bring knee up towards elbow

2. Pause then return to starting position and repeat for the other side

3. Ensure core is fully crunched at the top of the rep to bring knee even closer to elbow

Targets: Back (Latissimus Dorsi and Rhomboids), Trapezius and Thoracic Erector Spinae

STANDING LONG JUMP

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed, only a big space

Instructions:

3 sets of 8, rest between sets: 30s

1. Lower yourself into a squat position with feet shoulder-width apart

2. Swing arms back and use them to propel yourself forward

3. Bring legs forward for additional momentum

4. Jump as far as you can and land on the soles of your feet

Targets: Glutes, Hip Flexors, Quadriceps, Abs, Calves and Hamstrings

BURPEES

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 20, rest between sets: 30s

1. Start with feet shoulder-width apart and squat down until your thighs are parallel to the floor

2. From bottom of the squat, place your hands on the floor and kick your legs out behind you into a push up position

3. Push up until your arms are straight and tuck in your legs at the bottom of the squat position

4. Drive upwards through your heels until you are off the floor then repeat

Targets: Legs, Hips, Glutes, Abdomen, Arms, Chest and Shoulders

HANDSTAND WALL WALK

ACCESSIBILITY: Use any wall near you!

Instructions:

3 sets of 8 times your full body height, rest between sets 30s

1. Position yourself in a handstand position with your feet planted against a wall

2. Move hands forward and walk down the wall until you reach the bottom then repeat

Targets: Back (Deltoids), Shoulders, Core and Triceps

FROZEN V-SIT

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 8 5-10s hold, rest between sets: 60s

1. Lie on your back with arms and legs outstretched and your hands and feet lifted just above the floor

2. Begin the exercise by simultaneously raising your torso and legs up to touch your feet

3. Hold for 5 to 10 seconds

Targets: Rectus Abdominis, External and Internal Obliques, and Hip Flexors

SINGLE-LEG GLUTE BRIDGE

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 14, rest between sets: 30s

1. Lie on your back with 1 leg raised in the air

2. Thrust forward and raise your hips off the ground as high as you can

3. Slowly lower yourself to the floor

4. Clench glutes at the top of the rep to activate more muscle fibres and see greater growth

Targets: Hamstrings, Lower Back, Abs, Glutes

SIT UPS

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 14, rest between sets: 30s

1. Lie on the floor with your knees bent

2. Place hands behind your head and tense your core as you lift your torso up so your body forms a V shape with your thighs

3. Lower under control back to starting position

Targets: Abdominal Muscles (Rectus Abdominis, Transverse Abdominis and Obliques), Hip Flexors, Chest and Neck

MOUNTAIN CLIMBERS

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed, probably only shoes in order to prevent injury

Instructions:

3 sets of 1 min, rest between sets: 30s

1. Drop into a push up position with arms completely straight. Brace core as if you are about to be punched in the gut

2. Without changing lower-back posture, life right foot off the floor and raise knee to your chest

3. Touch the floor with your right toes. Simultaneously jumping your right food back to starting position while bringing your left knee to your chest this time. Alternate back and forth

Targets: Glutes, Legs, Triceps, Shoulders and Back

SIDE PLANKS

ACCESSIBILITY: No equipment needed

Instructions:

3 sets of 4 30s, rest between sets: 30s

1. Lie on the left side of your body with knees straight and prop your upper body up to take its weight on your forearm.

2. Brace your core and raise your hips until your body forms a straight line.

3. Hold this position while breathing deeply

4. Roll over and repeat on the other side

Targets: Oblique Abdominal Muscles
