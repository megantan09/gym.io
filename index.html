Bodyweight Shark's Past

Founded in 2021 by Megan Tan, a Singaporean teenager that has always been very involved in sports since her early days, Bodyweight Shark focuses on bodyweight exercises that can be done at home. Her purpose of starting this company was due to the current COVID-19 pandemic occurring worldwide, disallowing many people to have access to gyms. Even if there are access to public and even private gyms, these gyms have very limited slots to put in place social distancing measures. It is also possibly a location where people can spread viruses to one another due to shared equipments and being in an enclosed area.
